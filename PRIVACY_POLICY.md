# Privacy Policy for Splytt

**Last Updated: December 21, 2025**

## Introduction

Splytt ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Splytt ("the App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information Collected Automatically

When you use Splytt, we automatically collect certain information:

- **Device Identifier**: A randomly generated unique identifier (UUID) stored locally on your device to personalize your experience
- **Video Viewing History**: Information about which videos you have watched to prevent showing duplicate content
- **Content Preferences**: Hashtag preferences and confidence scores derived from your viewing behavior to improve content recommendations
- **Usage Data**: General app usage patterns to improve our service

### Information You Provide

- **Uploaded Content**: If you choose to upload videos, we collect the video content and associated metadata (hashtags, descriptions)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- **Personalize Your Feed**: Recommend videos based on your viewing history and preferences
- **Prevent Duplicate Content**: Track watched videos to show you fresh content
- **Improve Our Service**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance the app experience
- **Process Uploads**: Store and distribute content you choose to share

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Your viewing history, preferences, and device identifier are stored locally on your device using secure storage
- **Cloud Storage**: Uploaded videos are stored on Amazon Web Services (AWS) S3 servers located in the United States
- **Database**: Video metadata and anonymized user preferences are stored in AWS DynamoDB

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: We use AWS for hosting and storage services
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or to protect our rights

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Stored on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data
- **Viewing History**: Limited to the most recent 1,000 videos watched
- **Uploaded Content**: Retained until you request deletion

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request information about data we have collected
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your data by contacting us
- **Opt-Out**: Uninstall the app to stop all data collection

## Children's Privacy

Splytt is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

- Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
- AWS security infrastructure for cloud storage
- Local device encryption for stored preferences

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

- **Amazon Web Services (AWS)**: Cloud infrastructure and storage
- **Expo**: App development and update distribution

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.
